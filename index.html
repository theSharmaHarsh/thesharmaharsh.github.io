About Me

I am a Research Fellow at Department of Architecture and Planning, Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Roorkee, India under the National Programme on Technology Enhanced Learning (NPTEL) Pre-doctoral Fellowship Program.

My research interests lie in urban economics and trasnportation systems, with a focus on transport infrastructure planning and multimodal mobility, and urban form - transport interactions in Indian cities. I am interested in applied urban and transport policy questions, including accessibility, equity, and the role of transport systems in shaping urban development and mobility outcomes in rapidly growing cities.
