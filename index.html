I am a Research Fellow at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Roorkee under the NPTEL Pre-Doctoral Fellowship. My research focuses on urban economics, urban planning, and transportation systems, with an emphasis on empirical analysis of urban form, mobility, and infrastructure impacts in rapidly urbanizing contexts.

My work aims to contribute to evidence-based urban and transport policy, particularly in emerging economies.

My research interests lie in urban transportation systems and urban economics, with a focus on housing and transport affordability, land use and urban form, and sustainable urban mobility.

I use applied econometric and spatial analysis methods, combining statistical analysis in R with GIS-based spatial analysis using ArcGIS and QGIS.
