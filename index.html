About Me

I am a Research Fellow at IIT Roorkee with an academic background in economics, urban planning, and transportation systems. My work focuses on applied research in urban transport, housing, and infrastructure finance, with an emphasis on evidence-based policy and quantitative analysis.

Research Interests

My primary research interests include Bus Rapid Transit Systems (BRTS), urban transportation planning, multimodal mobility, urban form and land use, infrastructure finance, municipal bonds, cost-benefit analysis, and the economic evaluation of public transport systems in Indian cities.
